HOSTELS

There are a total of five hostles in our school compound..Each hostel has two wings distant from which other..Each wing can accommodate a mass total of 800 students

The two wings have been designed in such a way that they can satisfy the gender currently residing in them...One wing is for females the other for males hence accommodating a total of 1600 plus in each hostel.
